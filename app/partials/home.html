The Saturday Neighborhood Health Clinic

For Patients

We provide basic health care to uninsured members of our community completely free of cost, with the goal of taking care of our patients' immediate needs while helping them find a long-term primary care physician.

For Physicians

The SNHC is typically staffed by one attending physician at each clinic date. That physician is responsible for both mentoring the students and for the high-level care of the patients that day.

View details »

For Students

The SNHC is a student-run clinic. We rely on student volunteers to staff our clinic each week. In return, we help them hone their skills—from history-taking to mentoring other students—while giving back to the community.
